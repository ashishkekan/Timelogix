How to Use This Calculator

  • Enter your report data: Total Net Hours, Lunch Hours, and Present Days from your monthly report
  • Enter today's details: Your arrival time (lunch is fixed at 30 minutes)
  • Optionally enter your Desired Average working hours per day
  • Click "Calculate Required Hours" to see:
    • Your current average working hours
    • Required working hours for today
    • Exact time you should leave the office
  • For projected average: Enter a planned departure time and click "Calculate Projected Average" to see what your average would be
  • All times use your office format (e.g., 8 hours 15 minutes = 8.15)
